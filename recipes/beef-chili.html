A simple Beef Chili

Description

This homemade chili is loaded with beef, beans, and crowd-pleasing flavor.

Ingredient List:

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Kidney Beans
  • Stewed Tomatoes
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook the beef and the onion.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients, then bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce the heat and let simmer for 15 minutes.
